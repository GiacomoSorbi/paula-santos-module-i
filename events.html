Poetry and Philosophy Conference

When: 16-17 March 2020
Organiser: University of Warwick

The University of Warwick will host this BSA Synergy conference focusing on questions of shared interest for poetry and philosophy. Poets, philosophers, philosopher-poets, poet-philosophers, and poetry scholars will bring their expertise and experience to the conversation.
